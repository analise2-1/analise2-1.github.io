Instructions:

  1. Find a photo of what you want or draw it
  2. Draw lines of where the cuts will be on the glass
  3. Find your glass
  4. Cut the peice of paper out using the lines you drew
  5. Glue the paper peices onto the glass and cut it out
  6. Repeat until done
  7. Go to the glass sander and sand the sides to be smooth or fit the paper better
  8. repeat until done
  9. Wait until dry or dry it yourself
  10. put on copper foil on every glass peice
  11. use the bone tool to smooth out the foil
  12. repeat until done
  13. paint the foil in flux so the metal can stick
  14. put the peices together
  15. with the soldering iron melt the metal on top of the foil
  16. repeat until peice is finished
  17. let dry then clean off with water and soap
  18. Congradulations your done!!
